Crypto Currency Define

Cryptocurrency is a digital or virtual currency that employs cryptography for security purposes, making it difficult to counterfeit or double-spend. It is decentralized in nature, operating on a technology called blockchain, which is a distributed ledger enforced by a network of computers or nodes.
